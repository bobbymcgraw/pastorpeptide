### 🔹 **What is Testagen?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from testicular tissue (Khavinson-style oral bioregulator).
**Mechanism:**

- Signals to support the testes' own regenerative and hormone-producing capacity rather than directly supplying exogenous hormone.
- Associated with support for natural testosterone output, sperm quality/motility, and prostate tissue health.
- Carries mild immune and metabolic support effects reported alongside its reproductive-axis activity.

**Primary focus:**

- Male hormonal and reproductive health — testosterone tone, fertility support, and prostate maintenance (including BPH-related complaints).
- General male vitality: energy, libido, and metabolic function tied to healthy testosterone levels.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:**

- **100 mcg per capsule**, generally **1–2 capsules, 1–2 times daily**.

**Frequency:**

- Daily dosing for a minimum course of **20 days**.

**Rationale:** As an oral bioregulator, Testagen is designed for GI-stable short-peptide signaling rather than systemic hormone replacement — the goal is nudging the testes' own regulatory machinery, not overriding it.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** Minimum 20-day course; may be repeated or cycled based on how an individual's hormonal picture responds.
**Off-cycle:** Reassess hormone panels between courses before repeating; not intended as continuous daily use indefinitely.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Taken with or around meals per label convention for oral bioregulators.
- Best framed as a structured short course rather than an ongoing daily supplement.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral)**

- **Example protocol:** 100 mcg, 1 capsule twice daily, for 20 days, with hormone monitoring before and after the course.
- Often discussed alongside **Testoluten** (also testicular-tissue derived) for a fuller hypothalamic-pituitary-testicular axis approach, or paired with general vitality-support bioregulators.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Testagen targets **male reproductive and hormonal function**, complementary stacks tend to focus on the broader HPTA and prostate picture:
🔸 **Reproductive Axis Support:**

- **Testoluten:** Overlapping testicular-tissue bioregulator, sometimes discussed together for hormone axis regulation.

🔸 **General Vitality & Recovery:**

- **Immune- and metabolism-supportive bioregulators:** Complement Testagen's mild systemic effects without duplicating its reproductive-axis mechanism.

**Rationale:** Testagen's role is narrow and organ-specific — the male reproductive axis — so stacking logic centers on either reinforcing that axis or supporting general resilience alongside it.
