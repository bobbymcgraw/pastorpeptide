### 🔹 **What is Normoftal?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator (cytomax) derived from retinal tissue, part of the Khavinson-style Russian peptide bioregulator family.
**Mechanism:**

- Signals retinal cells toward repair and regeneration, supporting the eye's functional capacity over time.
- Shields retinal neurons from oxidative stress and the cumulative inflammatory load associated with aging or environmental exposure.
- Improves microcirculation to the retina, supporting nutrient and oxygen delivery to a tissue that depends heavily on local blood flow.

**Primary focus:**

- General retinal health and support for visual acuity, particularly relevant to macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and other degenerative eye conditions.
- Secondary support against eye strain from prolonged screen exposure and UV load.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** Commonly dosed at **100 mcg per capsule**, with **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily**.
**Typical protocol:** A **30-day course**, often repeated periodically to maintain retinal support over the long term.
**Rationale:** The oral route is the practical entry point for ongoing retinal maintenance; the peptide's short structure allows it to survive digestion and act on retinal tissue signaling.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 30 days per course.
**Off-cycle:** Cycled use with rest periods between courses is the standard approach, consistent with other bioregulators in this family.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- No strong timing preference is emphasized; consistency across the 30-day window matters more than time of day.
- Some initial dryness or irritation has been reported early in a course, along with temporary shifts in visual adjustment as blood flow to the retina changes — both are described as short-lived.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral capsule)**

- **Example protocol:** 100 mcg capsule, 1–2 capsules daily, for 30 days, repeated periodically (e.g., a few times per year) for ongoing eye health maintenance.
- Often discussed alongside Retinalamin given the shared focus on retinal tissue.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Normoftal is focused on **retinal repair, neuroprotection, and microcirculation**, complementary stacking tends to lean toward other vision-support and antioxidant-oriented compounds:
🔸 **Vision-Focused Bioregulators:**

- **Retinalamin:** Overlapping retinal-tissue focus, often discussed as a complementary or alternative option depending on route preference (oral vs. injectable).

🔸 **General Antioxidant & Neuroprotective Support:**

- **Pinealon:** Broader neuroprotective bioregulator that may pair conceptually given Normoftal's neuroprotective angle within retinal tissue specifically.

**Rationale:** Normoftal targets the retina specifically; stacking aims to **reinforce neuroprotection and circulatory support** without duplicating the same localized mechanism.
