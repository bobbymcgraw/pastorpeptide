### 🔹 **What is Methylene Blue?**

**Class:** Synthetic phenothiazine dye; redox-active compound with mitochondrial, neuroprotective, and metabolic applications.
**Mechanism:**

- Acts as an **alternative electron carrier** in the mitochondrial electron transport chain, bypassing complex I/III dysfunction → improves **ATP production**.
- Functions as a **potent antioxidant** (scavenges nitric oxide and reactive oxygen species).
- Enhances **cytochrome oxidase activity** → boosts cellular respiration and oxygen utilization.
- Low doses show **nootropic and antidepressant effects**; high doses can act as a monoamine oxidase (MAO) inhibitor.

**Primary focus:**

- Cognitive enhancement, neuroprotection, and mood support.
- Mitochondrial health and anti-aging.
- Potential antimicrobial (historically used as an antimalarial and urinary antiseptic).
- Adjunct in photodynamic therapy and low-dose metabolic support.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage (most common)**
**Range:**

- **Low-dose (nootropic/mitochondrial support):** 0.5–4 mg/kg daily (typical microdosing is ~0.5–2 mg/kg).
- **High-dose (clinical/antimicrobial use):** 5–10 mg/kg (rarely used outside medical supervision).

**Notes:** Oral administration is the most common for nootropic and metabolic purposes.

---

🔹 **Injectable Dosage (medical use only)**
**Range:**

- **IV (for methemoglobinemia):** 1–2 mg/kg over 5 minutes, may repeat after 1 hour (max 7 mg/kg total).
- Rarely used in research/anti-aging communities due to risks and medical restrictions.

**Rationale:** Injection is strictly therapeutic and carries risk of serotonin syndrome in patients on SSRIs.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**

- **Low-dose cognitive/metabolic support:** Often used continuously or in cycles of 4–12 weeks.
- **Off-cycle:** Some users take 1–2 weeks off after 2–3 months of continuous use.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- **Morning or midday** is favored to avoid insomnia and to align with energy-demanding periods.
- Should be taken away from SSRIs/SNRIs to avoid serotonin syndrome risk.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral)**

- Example protocol: 1 mg/kg oral dose each morning for 8 weeks.
- Paired with: Mitochondrial support stack (CoQ10, PQQ, NAD⁺ precursors, ALA).

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Methylene Blue acts as a **mitochondrial redox enhancer and cognitive support agent**, stacks emphasize **energy metabolism, neuroprotection, and longevity**:
🔸 **Mitochondrial & Energy Support:**

- **CoQ10 + PQQ:** Synergize with MB’s electron transport support.
- **NAD⁺ precursors (NMN, NR) or direct NAD⁺ therapy:** Complement ATP generation.
- **L-Carnitine:** Enhances fatty acid transport into mitochondria.

🔸 **Cognitive Enhancement & Neuroprotection:**

- **Noopept, Dihexa, Semax:** Support synaptic plasticity alongside MB’s metabolic boost.
- **Lion’s Mane or BDNF enhancers:** Pair well for long-term neurogenesis.

🔸 **Anti-Aging & Longevity:**

- **Rapalogs, Epitalon, FOXO4-DRI:** Address cellular senescence while MB maintains mitochondrial efficiency.
- **Antioxidants (ALA, NAC, Vitamin C):** Reinforce MB’s redox balancing effects.

---

⚠️ **Safety Notes:**

- **Contraindicated** with SSRIs, SNRIs, or other serotonergic drugs (risk of serotonin syndrome).
- High doses may cause **urinary burning, GI upset, or blue discoloration of skin/urine**.
