### 🔹 **What is Adalank?**

**Class:** N-Acetyl Selank Amidate — a structurally modified variant of **Selank**, the tuftsin-analog anxiolytic/nootropic heptapeptide.
**Mechanism:**

- Shares Selank's core mechanism: modulates the **GABAergic system** for anxiolytic effects, supports **BDNF expression** for cognitive resilience, and carries immunomodulatory activity tied to the tuftsin backbone.
- The **N-acetyl and amidate modifications** are intended to improve resistance to enzymatic degradation, extending duration of action compared to standard Selank — the same logic behind NA Semax Amidate's relationship to standard Semax.
- Marketed and discussed in the research community as an enhanced, longer-acting version of Selank rather than a distinct pathway. See this site's **Selank** page for the standard, unmodified version this compound is derived from.

**Primary focus:**

- Anxiety reduction (social/generalized) with a non-sedating profile.
- Mood stabilization and stress resilience.
- Cognitive support (focus, learning) layered on top of its anxiolytic effect.
- Longer-duration alternative for those seeking less frequent dosing than standard Selank requires.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage:** Not practical; poor oral bioavailability consistent with the base Selank molecule. Intranasal delivery is standard.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Nasal Dosage (most common)**
**Range:**

- Intranasal dosing typically discussed in the **200–600 mcg per dose** range, generally on the lower/less-frequent end relative to standard Selank given its extended duration.

**Frequency:**

- Often dosed **once or twice daily**, less frequently than standard Selank's typical 2–3x daily schedule.

**Rationale:** Intranasal delivery allows direct nose-to-brain uptake; the amidated, N-acetylated modification is intended to reduce how often redosing is needed to maintain anxiolytic effect.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 2–4 weeks of daily use, consistent with typical Selank-family research protocols.
**Off-cycle:** Periodic breaks commonly discussed, particularly if used situationally for stress rather than as continuous daily use.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (nasal)**

- **Example protocol:** 300 mcg intranasal once daily, used situationally or over a 3–4 week window, chosen over standard Selank specifically for reduced dosing frequency.
- Sometimes discussed alongside **NA Semax Amidate** for a combined anxiolytic-plus-cognitive research approach, mirroring how standard Selank and Semax are often paired given their shared Pro-Gly-Pro motif.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Adalank shares Selank's **anxiolytic/nootropic mechanism**, complementary stacks follow the same logic as standard Selank:
🔸 **Cognitive Pairing:**

- **Semax or NA Semax Amidate:** Complementary BDNF/dopaminergic cognitive support alongside Selank-family anxiolytic effects.

🔸 **General Nootropic Support:**

- Other stress-resilience or mood-focused peptides may be layered depending on the specific research goal.

**Rationale:** As an enhanced-duration Selank variant, its stacking logic mirrors standard Selank's — the main practical difference is frequency of dosing, not what it pairs well with.
