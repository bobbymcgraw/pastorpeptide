## 🔹 What is 5-Amino-1MQ?

- **Class:** Small molecule (not technically a peptide, but often grouped in biohacking circles).
- **Mechanism:** Inhibits **nicotinamide N-methyltransferase (NNMT)**, which helps preserve intracellular NAD⁺ levels.
- **Primary focus:** Fat loss, metabolic support, improved mitochondrial efficiency, and potentially anti-aging applications.

---

## 🔹 Oral Dosage (most common)

- **Range studied/used in research:** **50–150 mg per day**
- **Typical protocol:**
  - 50 mg **2–3x daily** with meals.
  - Split dosing helps maintain steady exposure due to limited half-life.
- **Notes:** Oral bioavailability is decent, making this the most common route.

---

## 🔹 Injectable Dosage (subcutaneous or IM – less common)

- **Range:** **5–10 mg daily** (occasionally up to 15 mg/day in experimental settings).
  - Recommend titrating up from **1mg daily** to determine your tolerance and any side effects. Increase dosage as needed.
- **Frequency:** Once daily or every other day, depending on goal and tolerance.
- **Rationale:** Lower doses are used vs oral due to bypassing first-pass metabolism.

---

## 🔹 Cycle Length

- **Common cycle length:** **4–8 weeks**
- **Off-cycle:** At least 2–4 weeks off before restarting, to prevent desensitization or adaptation.
- Many stackers will run it alongside metabolic agents like **AOD-9604, Tesofensine, or GLP-1 agonists** in fat-loss protocols.

---

## 🔹 Optimal Dosing Window

- **Oral:**
  - Morning dose (fasted or with light meal)
  - Midday dose (to sustain NAD⁺/energy)
  - Optional early afternoon dose (avoid evening if sleep disruption occurs).
- **Injectable:**
  - Once daily, typically morning or pre-workout for metabolic boost.

## 🔹 Stacking Strategy (Educational)

Since 5-Amino-1MQ works by **preserving NAD⁺** and **enhancing metabolism**, stacks should complement this:

### 🔸 Fat Loss Synergy

- **AOD-9604** – enhances lipolysis, synergizes for adipose targeting.
- **Tesofensine or GLP-1 agonists (semaglutide, liraglutide)** – appetite control + metabolic rate support.
- **GH fragments (CJC-1295/Ipamorelin)** – improve fat loss & muscle retention.

### 🔸 NAD⁺ / Mitochondrial Support

- **NMN or NR** – additional NAD⁺ precursors, amplified by NNMT inhibition.
- **Resveratrol or Pterostilbene** – sirtuin activators to leverage NAD⁺ gains.
- **Methylene Blue (low-dose)** – mitochondrial redox enhancer.

### 🔸 Anti-Aging / Energy Stack

- 5-Amino-1MQ + **NAD⁺ precursor** (NMN/NR) + **sirtuin activator** (resveratrol)
- Optional add-on: **MOTS-c** (mitochondrial peptide) for synergistic endurance and metabolic health.
