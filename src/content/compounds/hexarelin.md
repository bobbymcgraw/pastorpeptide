### 🔹 **What is Hexarelin?**

**Class:** Synthetic hexapeptide, **GHRP (Growth Hormone Releasing Peptide)**.
**Mechanism:**

- Potent **GH secretagogue** that binds to the ghrelin receptor (GHS-R1a), stimulating endogenous growth hormone release.
- Increases pulsatile GH secretion more robustly than GHRP-6 or GHRP-2 in many users.
- May influence appetite modestly via ghrelin receptor activation.

**Primary focus:**

- Growth hormone release for tissue repair, recovery, and anti-aging effects.
- Supports muscle growth, fat metabolism, improved sleep quality, and recovery from training or injury.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage:** Not practical; peptide degraded in GI tract. Only injectable/subcutaneous is effective.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Subcutaneous Dosage (most common)**
**Range:**

- Typical dosing is **100–300 µg per injection**, 1–3 times daily depending on goals and GH secretagogue stacking.

**Frequency:**

- Often dosed **pre-workout or pre-bedtime** to take advantage of natural GH pulses.

**Rationale:** Hexarelin is potent at small microgram doses; subcutaneous injection allows for predictable GH stimulation without high peptide exposure.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 4–8 weeks for recovery, anti-aging, or training support.
**Off-cycle:** 2–4 weeks recommended to avoid receptor desensitization and maintain responsiveness.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- **Pre-bedtime:** Enhances natural nocturnal GH pulse and may improve sleep quality.
- **Pre-workout:** Optional for post-exercise recovery benefits.
- Avoid daytime dosing in sensitive individuals to reduce potential mild GH-related water retention or fatigue.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (injectable / subcutaneous)**

- **Example protocol:** 150 µg Hexarelin pre-bedtime daily for 6 weeks, followed by a 2-week break.
- Can be stacked with **CJC-1295 (with or without DAC)** or **Ipamorelin** for synergistic GH pulse enhancement.
