### 🔹 **What is Libidon?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator (cytomax) derived from prostate tissue, part of the Khavinson-style Russian peptide bioregulator family.
**Mechanism:**

- Signals prostate tissue toward repair and normal cellular turnover, which is the basis for its traditional use around benign prostatic changes.
- Supports healthy testosterone production and broader reproductive hormone balance.
- Carries a mild immune-modulating, anti-inflammatory action localized to the prostate gland.

**Primary focus:**

- Prostate tissue health and urinary comfort in men navigating age-related prostate changes.
- Secondary support for libido, erectile function, and sperm quality tied to healthier testosterone status.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** Commonly dosed at **20 mg per capsule**, with **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily**, roughly 15–20 minutes before meals.
**Typical protocol:** A **30-day course** is standard; more involved cases are sometimes discussed at higher intakes of up to 2–6 capsules daily, though this escalates well past the routine maintenance dose.
**Rationale:** The oral capsule form is the practical route for ongoing use; Libidon's short peptide structure is designed to survive digestion and act locally on prostate signaling.

---

🔹 **Subcutaneous Injection (less common)**
**Range:** Some protocols describe **10–20 mg daily** by subcutaneous injection, scaled to the severity of the underlying prostate concern.
**Rationale:** Injectable use shows up in more clinically directed protocols rather than routine self-directed use; oral remains the far more common entry point.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 30 days is the typical single course.
**Off-cycle:** Bioregulators in this family are used as periodic short courses rather than continuously; a rest interval between courses is standard.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Taken **shortly before meals** (roughly 15–20 minutes prior) rather than with food, which is a distinguishing detail from many other bioregulators in this family.
- Hormonal shifts, particularly in testosterone, are worth monitoring over a course given Libidon's endocrine-adjacent mechanism.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral capsule)**

- **Example protocol:** 20 mg capsule, 1–2 capsules daily before meals, for 30 days, used as part of a broader prostate-health and hormonal-support routine.
- Often discussed alongside other reproductive and hormonal-support peptides.

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Libidon is focused on **prostate health and male hormonal balance**, complementary stacking tends to lean toward reproductive and metabolic support:
🔸 **Reproductive & Hormonal Bioregulators:**

- **Testagen, Testoluten:** Testicular and reproductive-tissue-focused bioregulators that may complement Libidon's hormonal-balance emphasis.

- **BPC-157:** Broader anti-inflammatory support that may pair conceptually with Libidon's localized anti-inflammatory action in the prostate.

**Rationale:** Libidon targets prostate tissue and testosterone balance specifically; stacking aims to **reinforce the broader male reproductive-hormonal axis** without duplicating the same mechanism.
