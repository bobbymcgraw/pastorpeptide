### 🔹 **What is Endoluten?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from pineal gland tissue, part of the Khavinson-style Russian organ-specific bioregulator line.
**Mechanism:**

- Helps balance melatonin production, supporting healthier circadian rhythm regulation and sleep patterns.
- Reduces oxidative stress, contributing to cellular renewal and a general anti-aging effect.
- Supports immune resilience alongside endocrine function, particularly relevant to hormonal balance in aging individuals.

**Primary focus:**

- Longevity and cellular regeneration, sleep quality and circadian rhythm optimization, and hormonal/endocrine balance support, especially in older populations.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** **20 mg per capsule**, typically **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily**.
**Timing:** Commonly taken **15–20 minutes before meals**.
**Typical protocol:** A standard course runs **30 days**, with periodic repeat cycles recommended for sustained benefit.
**Rationale:** The short peptide structure allows oral dosing to target pineal-tissue signaling without requiring injection.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 30 days per course.
**Off-cycle:** Periodic cycling over time, rather than continuous indefinite use, is the more commonly reported pattern for longevity-focused maintenance.

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Dosing **15–20 minutes before meals** is the standard timing referenced.
- Given the melatonin-regulation angle, some users align dosing with morning and evening routines to support circadian rhythm.

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Endoluten is centered on **pineal function, melatonin regulation, and longevity**, complementary stacking tends to focus on circadian and anti-aging support:
🔸 **Circadian / Longevity-Focused Bioregulators:**

- **Epitalon:** Telomerase-activating peptide with an overlapping pineal and circadian focus, commonly paired with Endoluten.

🔸 **Hormonal / Endocrine Support:**

- **Glandokort:** Adrenal-focused bioregulator sometimes combined for broader endocrine balance alongside Endoluten's pineal-focused effects.

**Rationale:** Endoluten targets pineal gland function specifically; stacking aims to **reinforce circadian health and broader hormonal balance** without duplicating mechanisms unnecessarily.
