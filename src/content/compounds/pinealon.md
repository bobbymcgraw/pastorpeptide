### 🔹 **What is Pinealon?**

**Class:** Synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Arg), part of the Khavinson-style Russian peptide bioregulator family, focused on brain metabolism and neuroprotection.
**Mechanism:**

- Reduces oxidative stress at the neuronal level, protecting brain cells from cumulative damage.
- Modulates neurotransmitter activity in ways associated with improved memory, focus, and learning capacity.
- Influences gene expression tied to neuronal repair and synaptic plasticity.

**Primary focus:**

- Cognitive support — memory, recall, and mental clarity — with a secondary neuroprotective angle relevant to age-related cognitive decline.
- Possible contribution to mood stability and reduced mental fatigue.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** Commonly dosed at **20 mg per capsule**, with **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily**, generally with food.
**Typical protocol:** A standard **30-day course**, repeatable in cycles depending on ongoing cognitive-support goals.
**Rationale:** Pinealon's short peptide structure allows it to survive oral administration and act on brain-metabolism pathways without needing injection.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 30 days per course.
**Off-cycle:** Cycled use with breaks between courses is standard for this category, rather than continuous daily use.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Taking doses with food is the commonly reported approach.
- Mild headaches or temporary fatigue have occasionally been noted, generally described as short-lived.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral capsule)**

- **Example protocol:** 20 mg capsule, 1–2 capsules daily with food, for 30 days, repeated in cycles as needed for ongoing cognitive support.
- Often discussed alongside other neuroprotective and longevity-oriented peptides.

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Pinealon is focused on **neuroprotection, cognitive enhancement, and gene expression related to neuronal repair**, complementary stacking tends to lean toward broader brain-health and longevity support:
🔸 **Cognitive & Neuroprotective Support:**

- **Semax, Selank, Cerebrolysin:** Established nootropic and neuroprotective peptides that may complement Pinealon's cognitive-support mechanism.

- **Epitalon:** General cellular longevity peptide often discussed alongside brain-focused bioregulators.

**Rationale:** Pinealon targets brain metabolism and cognitive function specifically; stacking aims to **reinforce broader neurological health and longevity** across complementary pathways.
