### 🔹 **What is Svetinorm?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator (cytomax) derived from liver tissue, part of the Khavinson-style Russian peptide bioregulator family.
**Mechanism:**

- Normalizes intracellular metabolism within the liver, supporting its role in digestion, blood purification, and detoxification.
- Enhances the liver's capacity to filter and clear harmful substances, reducing toxin buildup.
- Supports lipid metabolism, carbohydrate processing, and blood sugar balance.
- Extends support to interconnected systems, including digestive, immune, and skin detoxification pathways.

**Primary focus:**

- Hepatic health and disease prevention, discussed in the context of hepatitis, cirrhosis, hepatosis, and toxin-induced liver damage.
- Secondary support for metabolic balance, skin conditions tied to liver dysfunction, and — in severe hepatic dysfunction — the neurological complications of hepatic encephalopathy.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** Commonly dosed at **21.5 mg per capsule**, with **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily**.
**Typical protocol:** A standard **30-day course**, with repeat courses considered every **3–6 months** for chronic liver conditions.
**Rationale:** As with other bioregulators in this family, Svetinorm is designed to survive oral administration and act locally on hepatic tissue signaling.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 30 days per course, repeated every 3–6 months for ongoing or chronic liver support.
**Off-cycle:** Cycled short courses with rest periods between them are the standard pattern rather than continuous use.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Mild GI discomfort during initial detoxification and occasional mild headaches during the adjustment period have both been reported, generally described as short-lived.
- Periodic liver enzyme monitoring is a sensible practice for anyone running extended or repeated courses.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral capsule)**

- **Example protocol:** 21.5 mg capsule, 1–2 capsules daily, for 30 days, repeated every 3–6 months for chronic hepatic support.
- Often discussed alongside other liver-focused bioregulators depending on the specific mechanism being emphasized.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Svetinorm is focused on **hepatic function restoration, detoxification, and metabolic regulation**, complementary stacking tends to lean toward other liver and metabolic-support compounds:
🔸 **Liver-Focused Bioregulators:**

- **Ovagen, Livagen:** Overlapping hepatic focus, often discussed together depending on whether direct regeneration or gene-level activity is being emphasized.

🔸 **Metabolic & Detox Support:**

- **Pielotax:** Kidney-focused bioregulator that complements Svetinorm's role in the body's broader filtration and detoxification capacity.

**Rationale:** Svetinorm targets hepatic tissue and systemic metabolic balance; stacking aims to **reinforce whole-body detoxification and metabolic health** across complementary organ-specific bioregulators.
