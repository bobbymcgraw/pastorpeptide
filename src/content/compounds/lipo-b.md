### 🔹 **What is Lipo-B?**

**Class:** Lipotropic injection blend — not technically a peptide, but grouped here because it's used interchangeably with peptides in metabolic and weight-management stacks. A related blend, **Lipo-C**, is also covered separately on this site.
**Mechanism:**

- Combines methionine, inositol, and choline (the core "MIC" lipotropic trio) with Vitamin B12 and Vitamin B6.
- Methionine and choline support fat mobilization and help prevent fat accumulation in the liver.
- Inositol supports fat and cholesterol metabolism and liver detoxification signaling.
- B12 and B6 support energy production, nervous system function, and red blood cell formation.

**Primary focus:**

- Supporting fat metabolism and mobilization as part of a broader weight-loss protocol.
- Liver detoxification and support for healthy fat processing.
- General energy and mood support via B-vitamin repletion.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage:** Not typically used; Lipo-B is formulated specifically as an injectable blend for direct systemic delivery.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Subcutaneous or Intramuscular Dosage (standard route)**
**Range:**

- Commonly dosed at **1–2 mL per injection**, 1–2 times weekly.

**Frequency:**

- Weekly or twice-weekly, often timed alongside other weight-loss protocol visits.

**Rationale:** Injectable delivery bypasses digestive breakdown of the B-vitamin and lipotropic components, ensuring more reliable systemic availability.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** Often used as an ongoing adjunct throughout an active weight-loss program rather than in short discrete cycles.
**Off-cycle:** No strict washout required; frequency is typically adjusted based on response and goals rather than mandated breaks.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Morning dosing is commonly preferred, since the B-vitamin component can have a mild energizing effect.
- Avoid evening dosing in those sensitive to the energy boost, as it may affect sleep.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (injectable)**

- **Example protocol:** 1 mL Lipo-B intramuscular weekly, used alongside a GLP-1 or other metabolic-support peptide protocol and paired with dietary and activity changes.
- Frequently used as a complementary add-on rather than a standalone weight-loss strategy.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Lipo-B supports fat metabolism and liver/energy function rather than acting as a hormone or appetite regulator, it pairs naturally with compounds that address those other levers:
🔸 **Weight Management Synergy:**

- **Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, Cagrilintide:** Appetite and glucose regulation that complements Lipo-B's fat-mobilization and liver-support role.

🔸 **Energy & Metabolic Support:**

- **NAD⁺ precursors (NMN, NR):** Support cellular energy production alongside Lipo-B's B-vitamin contribution.
- **CoQ10, L-Carnitine:** Support mitochondrial fat-burning capacity.

**Rationale:** Lipo-B fills the metabolic-support and liver-health gap in a stack rather than driving the primary weight-loss mechanism, which is why it's so often paired with GLP-1-class compounds.
**Studied in Humans?** Yes — lipotropic injections have a long history of clinical use, though mostly through practice-based evidence rather than large randomized trials of the combined formulation.
**FDA Approved?** The individual components (B12, B6, etc.) are FDA-approved as nutrients/drugs in their own right, but the fixed-dose Lipo-B combination itself is not an FDA-approved product.
**Allowed for 503a Compounding Pharmacy?** Yes — commonly compounded.
