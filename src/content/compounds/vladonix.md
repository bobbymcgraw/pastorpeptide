### 🔹 **What is Vladonix?**

**Class:** Peptide bioregulator derived from thymus tissue.
**Mechanism:**

- Enhances T-cell activity and production, supporting a stronger overall immune response.
- Modulates inflammatory cytokines, helping to reduce chronic inflammation and dial down immune overactivation where present.
- Supports both adaptive and innate immune pathways, improving the body's general capacity to fight off infection.

**Primary focus:**

- Immune system support for those with immune deficiencies, chronic infections, or general age-related immune decline.
- Autoimmune modulation — helping calm an overactive immune response — and general recovery support following illness or immune stress.
