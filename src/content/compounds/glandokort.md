### 🔹 **What is Glandokort?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from adrenal gland tissue, part of the Khavinson-style Russian organ-specific bioregulator line.
**Mechanism:**

- Supports repair and regeneration of adrenal cortical cells, contributing to improved hormonal production and balance.
- Modulates cortisol output, aiding the body's capacity to manage stress and recover from adrenal fatigue.
- Reduces inflammation within adrenal tissue, supporting long-term adrenal health and function.

**Primary focus:**

- Adrenal recovery in the context of chronic stress, burnout, or fatigue-related conditions, general hormonal balance support, and energy/metabolic function tied to healthy adrenal output.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** **20 mg per capsule**, typically **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily** with food.
**Typical protocol:** A standard course runs **30 days**, with repeat cycles considered based on individual health needs.
**Rationale:** The short peptide structure allows oral dosing to target adrenal cortical tissue without requiring injection.
