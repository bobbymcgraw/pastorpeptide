### 🔹 **What is Survodutide?**

Class: Synthetic 29-amino acid peptide; dual agonist of **glucagon** and **GLP-1 receptors**.
Mechanism:

- Activates **glucagon receptor (GCGR)** to enhance **lipid oxidation** and **energy expenditure**.
- Stimulates **GLP-1 receptor (GLP-1R)** to promote **satiety**, **insulin secretion**, and **glucose homeostasis**.
- Dual receptor activation leads to **synergistic effects** on **weight loss**, **fat metabolism**, and **cardiometabolic health**.
- Designed as a **glucagon analog** rather than an oxyntomodulin mimic, offering a distinct pharmacological profile.

Primary focus:

- **Weight loss** and **obesity management**.
- **Fat reduction**, particularly **visceral fat**.
- **Improvement in metabolic parameters** and **cardiovascular health**.
- Potential benefits in **non-alcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH)** and **metabolic dysfunction-associated steatohepatitis (MASH)**.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage**

- Not applicable; **poor oral bioavailability**.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Subcutaneous Dosage (most common)**
Range:

- **0.6 mg to 4.8 mg subcutaneously once weekly**.
- Doses are **up-titrated** to minimize **gastrointestinal side effects**.
- **6.0 mg** is the maximum dose investigated in clinical trials.

Rationale: Subcutaneous administration allows for **once-weekly dosing** with sustained receptor engagement.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**

- **46 weeks** in pivotal Phase 2 trials.
- Ongoing **Phase 3 trials** with extended durations to assess long-term efficacy and safety.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Administer on the **same day each week**, preferably in the **morning**.
- Consistent weekly dosing enhances **adherence** and **efficacy**.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example**
Example protocol: **2.4 mg subcutaneously once weekly** for **12 weeks**.
Paired with: **Lifestyle modifications** including **dietary changes** and **physical activity**.
Rationale: Supports **weight loss** and **metabolic health** in conjunction with behavioral interventions.

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Survodutide targets **dual metabolic pathways**, stacking strategies focus on enhancing **lipid metabolism**, **insulin sensitivity**, and **cardiovascular health**:
🔸 **Metabolic & Fat Loss Support**

- **Tesamorelin**: Enhances **GH release** for **fat reduction** and **muscle preservation**.
- **MOTS-c**: Improves **mitochondrial function** and **fat oxidation**.
- **L-Carnitine**: Facilitates **fatty acid transport** into mitochondria for **energy production**.

🔸 **Insulin Sensitivity & Cardiovascular Health**

- **Berberine**: Improves **insulin sensitivity** and supports **lipid metabolism**.
- **Omega-3 fatty acids**: Reduce **inflammation** and support **cardiovascular health**.

Rationale: Combining Survodutide with complementary agents can enhance **weight loss**, **metabolic function**, and **cardiovascular health**.

---

⚠️ **Safety Notes**

- **Gastrointestinal side effects** (e.g., nausea, diarrhea) are common, especially during dose escalation.
- Monitor for signs of **hypoglycemia**, particularly in patients with **type 2 diabetes**.
- Contraindicated in patients with a history of **medullary thyroid carcinoma** or **multiple endocrine neoplasia type 2**.
