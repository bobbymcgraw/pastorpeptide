### 🔹 **What is Glutathione ?**

**Class:** Endogenous tripeptide antioxidant composed of glutamate, cysteine, and glycine.
**Mechanism:** Acts as a major intracellular antioxidant, neutralizing reactive oxygen species (ROS) and free radicals. Supports detoxification pathways in the liver, maintains redox balance, and regenerates other antioxidants (e.g., vitamins C and E). Plays a role in immune modulation, cellular repair, and mitochondrial health.
**Primary focus:** Antioxidant support, detoxification, immune modulation, cellular repair, mitochondrial protection, and skin/anti-aging benefits.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage**
**Range studied/used in research:** 250–1000 mg daily. Oral bioavailability is moderate; liposomal formulations improve systemic absorption.
**Typical protocol:** 250–500 mg twice daily for general antioxidant support; higher doses (up to 1 g daily) may be used for liver detox or acute oxidative stress.
**Notes:** Liposomal or acetylated forms (e.g., N-acetylcysteine + glycine + glutamate precursors) enhance absorption compared to standard oral capsules.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Sublingual / IV Dosage (most common)**
**Range:** 200–600 mg per IM Injection 2–3 times per week for antioxidant or detoxification purposes. Sublingual or subcutaneous formulations are less common.
**Frequency:** 2–3 times per week; may be adjusted based on oxidative stress markers, liver function, or clinical goals.
**Rationale:** IM delivery bypasses gastrointestinal degradation and achieves higher plasma concentrations for acute detox or therapeutic effects.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 4–12 weeks depending on oxidative stress load, detox goals, or clinical protocols.
**Off-cycle:** Breaks of 1–2 weeks may be implemented to assess tolerance and systemic response.
**Note:** Long-term daily supplementation is generally considered safe for healthy individuals; dosing may be adjusted for specific therapeutic applications.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**
**Oral / Sublingual / IV:**

- Morning or early afternoon is generally preferred to coincide with active metabolic periods.
- Evening dosing is acceptable but may be paired with other antioxidants for maximal cellular repair.
