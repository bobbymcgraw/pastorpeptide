### 🔹 **What is Vesugen?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from vascular tissue.
**Mechanism:**

- Promotes regeneration of endothelial cells, supporting the elasticity and functional health of blood vessels.
- Enhances circulatory flow, helping reduce vascular resistance and inflammation along vessel walls.
- Carries anti-atherosclerotic properties, helping protect against arterial plaque buildup that contributes to hypertension and vascular disease.

**Primary focus:**

- Cardiovascular and circulatory health — improving blood vessel function to reduce risk factors tied to heart disease and stroke.
- Supporting oxygen and nutrient delivery to tissues via better vascular efficiency, and helping prevent complications from chronic conditions like hypertension and ischemia.
