### 🔹 **What is NAD⁺?**

**Class:** Essential coenzyme found in all living cells; central to redox reactions, mitochondrial function, and cellular signaling.
**Mechanism:**

- Functions as a **redox carrier** in metabolic pathways (glycolysis, TCA cycle, oxidative phosphorylation).
- Critical for **sirtuin and PARP activation** → influences DNA repair, mitochondrial biogenesis, and longevity pathways.
- NAD⁺ levels decline with age, contributing to metabolic slowdown, neurodegeneration, and impaired recovery.
- Restoring NAD⁺ supports **energy metabolism, cellular resilience, and healthy aging**.

**Primary focus:**

- Mitochondrial health and ATP production.
- DNA repair and anti-aging.
- Neuroprotection and cognitive support.
- Recovery from metabolic or physical stress.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage**
**Range:**

- NAD⁺ itself is poorly absorbed orally.
- **Precursors (NMN, NR, Niacinamide):** 250–1000 mg/day are common to raise NAD⁺ levels indirectly.

**Notes:** Direct oral NAD⁺ is not reliable; precursors are the preferred oral approach.

---

🔹 **Injectable / IV / Subcutaneous Dosage (most common for direct NAD⁺ use)**
**Range:**

- **IV infusions (clinical use):** 250–1000 mg per session, infused slowly over 1–4 hours.
- **Subcutaneous/IM research use:** 50–300 mg, 2–4x per week.
- **Low-dose optimization:** 40–100 mg 2–3x per week (anecdotal for energy and recovery).

**Rationale:** Direct delivery bypasses poor oral bioavailability and provides immediate mitochondrial support.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**

- **Wellness/anti-aging use:** Ongoing with weekly or monthly maintenance dosing.
- **Intensive support (fatigue, recovery, cognitive repair):** 4–8 weeks at higher frequency, followed by taper or maintenance.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- **Morning or early afternoon** → supports daytime energy, avoids overstimulation at night.
- IV sessions are typically scheduled **during the day** for better tolerance.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example**

- Example protocol: **100 mg NAD⁺ SC, 3x/week (Mon/Wed/Fri)** for 8 weeks.
- Paired with: **NMN 500 mg daily + CoQ10 + PQQ** for mitochondrial synergy.

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since NAD⁺ is a **master metabolic regulator**, stacking focuses on amplifying energy production, resilience, and repair:
🔸 **Mitochondrial & Energy Support:**

- **MOTS-c, AICAR, L-Carnitine:** Enhance fatty acid oxidation and ATP output alongside NAD⁺.
- **CoQ10 + PQQ:** Synergize in electron transport and mitochondrial biogenesis.

🔸 **Longevity & DNA Repair:**

- **Epitalon:** Complements sirtuin activity with telomere regulation.
- **FOXO4-DRI or Senolytics:** Cellular cleanup alongside NAD⁺-driven repair.
- **Resveratrol or Quercetin:** Sirtuin activators that leverage NAD⁺ availability.

🔸 **Neuroprotection & Cognitive Support:**

- **Methylene Blue, Semax, Dihexa:** Combine metabolic and synaptic support.
- **Lion’s Mane:** Stimulates NGF and works synergistically with NAD⁺-driven repair.

**Rationale:** NAD⁺ acts as a **foundational metabolic and repair molecule**, so stacks emphasize mitochondrial cofactors, senolytics, and cognitive enhancers.

---

⚠️ **Safety Notes:**

- **Rapid IV pushes** can cause flushing, chest tightness, or anxiety; slow infusion is key.
- High-dose NAD⁺ may cause headaches, GI upset, or fatigue in some users.
- Long-term human trials are limited—precursors (NMN/NR) have stronger safety data.
