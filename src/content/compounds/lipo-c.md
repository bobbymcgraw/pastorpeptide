### 🔹 **What is Lipo-C?**

**Class:** Injectable metabolic / fat-burning blend. Lipo-C is not a peptide but rather a lipotropic blend.
**Mechanism:**
**Example dosage from a popular vendor (verify your vendor's formulation):**

- **L-Carnitine (50 mg/mL):** Transports long-chain fatty acids into mitochondria for β-oxidation and energy production.
- **Choline Chloride (50 mg/mL):** Supports methylation, fat metabolism, and liver function.
- **Methionine (15 mg/mL):** Sulfur-containing amino acid that aids in detoxification, antioxidant defense, and lipid metabolism.
- **Dexpanthenol (Vitamin B5, 5 mg/mL):** Coenzyme A precursor that supports fatty acid metabolism and cellular energy production.

***Clinically Proven Effective Dosing Ranges for each ingredient are as follows:***

- ***Methionine****: usually 25–100mg per injection*
- ***Choline****: often 50–100mg*
- ***L-Carnitine****: clinical fat loss/metabolic doses are 250–1,000mg per injection, sometimes more for performance use.*
- ***B5 (Dexpanthenol)****: usually supportive at 10–25mg*

**Primary focus:**

- Enhance **fat metabolism and energy production**.
- Support liver health, methylation, and mitochondrial efficiency.
- Promote recovery and overall metabolic performance during training or calorie-restricted phases.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage:** Not effective; designed for **subcutaneous injection**.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Subcutaneous Dosage (most common)**
**Range:**

- **Typical dose:** 1–3 mL per injection, 2–3 times weekly.
- Can be titrated based on energy, fat metabolism goals, and tolerance.

**Frequency:**

- Often administered pre-workout or in the morning to optimize energy and fat utilization.

**Rationale:** Small doses of combined metabolic cofactors enhance mitochondrial fatty acid oxidation and systemic energy without high-volume injections.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 4–12 weeks, often used continuously during fat loss, energy support, or metabolic optimization phases.
**Off-cycle:** Optional 1–2 weeks to assess efficacy or rotate with other metabolic support compounds.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- **Morning or pre-workout:** Supports fat oxidation and energy availability during activity.
- Can be combined with meals or exercise for improved mitochondrial uptake.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example**

- **Example protocol:** 1 mL subcutaneous 2–3x weekly during a 6–8 week fat metabolism or metabolic support phase.
- Pair with: Aerobic or resistance training, NAD⁺ precursors (NMN/NR), CoQ10/PQQ, and a calorie-appropriate diet.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Lipo-C **enhances fat metabolism and energy production**, complementary stacks focus on **mitochondrial support, fat utilization, and recovery**:
🔸 **Metabolic & Mitochondrial Support:**

- **L-Carnitine (oral or injectable)** for enhanced fatty acid transport.
- **NAD⁺ precursors (NMN, NR), PQQ, CoQ10:** Improve mitochondrial efficiency.
- **AICAR or Cardarine (GW-501516):** Support endurance and fat oxidation.

🔸 **Recovery & Performance Support:**

- **B-complex vitamins, antioxidants (NAC, Vitamin C, CoQ10):** Reduce oxidative stress during metabolic activity.
- **Amino acids / protein supplementation:** Preserve lean mass during fat loss.

**Rationale:** Lipo-C provides a **targeted metabolic cofactor boost**; stacking enhances systemic fat oxidation, energy, and mitochondrial function without duplicating mechanisms.
