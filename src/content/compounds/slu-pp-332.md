### 🔹 **What is SLU-PP-332?**

Class: Synthetic small-molecule peptide analog; potent pan-agonist of estrogen-related receptors (ERRα, ERRβ, ERRγ).
Mechanism:

- Activates **ERRα** (EC₅₀ ≈ 98 nM), **ERRβ** (EC₅₀ ≈ 230 nM), and **ERRγ** (EC₅₀ ≈ 430 nM), with highest potency for ERRα.
- Enhances **mitochondrial function** and **cellular respiration** in skeletal muscle cell lines.
- Increases **fast oxidative skeletal muscle fibers (Type IIa)** and **exercise endurance** in mice in an ERRα-specific manner.
- Activates an acute aerobic exercise genetic response via inducing the expression of **DDIT4**.
- Mimics key effects of endurance exercise, such as enhanced energy metabolism, mitochondrial biogenesis, and fat oxidation, without influencing estrogen receptor pathways.

Primary focus:

- **Metabolic enhancement** and **fat loss**.
- **Mitochondrial biogenesis** and **muscle endurance**.
- **Exercise mimetic** effects without the need for physical activity.
- Potential applications in **metabolic disorders** and **anti-aging**.
