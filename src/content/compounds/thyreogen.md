### 🔹 **What is Thyreogen?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from thyroid gland tissue.
**Mechanism:**

- Supports repair and regeneration of thyroid cells, aiding overall gland health.
- Encourages balanced production of T3 and T4 rather than forcing hormone levels in one direction.
- Contributes to metabolic optimization — energy metabolism, weight regulation, and cognitive clarity that track with healthy thyroid output.

**Primary focus:**

- Thyroid function support in the context of hypothyroidism, Hashimoto's thyroiditis, and general endocrine maintenance.
- Symptom relief tied to thyroid dysfunction: fatigue, brain fog, weight gain, and temperature sensitivity.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:**

- **20 mg per capsule**, typically **1–2 capsules, 1–2 times daily**.

**Frequency:**

- Taken **15–20 minutes before meals**, for a course of **30 days**.

**Rationale:** The pre-meal timing convention is shared across this bioregulator line; the intent is consistent oral absorption rather than any special interaction with food.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** 30-day course.
**Off-cycle:** Can be cycled periodically for long-term thyroid support, with thyroid panel monitoring recommended between courses.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Pre-meal dosing, split across the day if using the twice-daily protocol.
- Best used as a periodic thyroid-support course alongside routine lab monitoring rather than an unmonitored daily habit.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral)**

- **Example protocol:** 20 mg, 1 capsule before breakfast and dinner, for 30 days, with thyroid panel checks before and after.
- Sometimes discussed alongside general metabolic and vascular-support bioregulators (e.g., **Ventfort**, **Vesugen**) for a broader endocrine-and-circulatory approach.

---

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Thyreogen is organ-specific to the **thyroid gland**, complementary stacks tend to focus on the broader metabolic and endocrine picture:
🔸 **Metabolic & Endocrine Support:**

- Complementary bioregulators targeting related metabolic pathways can be layered without duplicating Thyreogen's thyroid-specific mechanism.

🔸 **Vascular Support:**

- **Ventfort, Vesugen:** Support circulatory delivery, which can matter for overall endocrine and metabolic function.

**Rationale:** Thyreogen's role is narrowly the thyroid gland itself, so stacking logic is about supporting the broader metabolic and circulatory systems that depend on healthy thyroid signaling, not stacking multiple thyroid-specific agents.
