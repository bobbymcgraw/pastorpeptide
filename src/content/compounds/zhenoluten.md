### 🔹 **What is Zhenoluten?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from ovarian tissue.
**Mechanism:**

- Supports repair and regeneration of ovarian cells, aiming at reproductive longevity rather than acute hormone replacement.
- Helps balance estrogen and progesterone levels, which relates to improved menstrual regularity and fertility potential.
- Carries anti-inflammatory properties relevant to reproductive conditions such as PCOS and endometriosis.

**Primary focus:**

- Female reproductive health — supporting ovarian function, fertility, and hormonal regulation across the menstrual cycle.
- Symptom relief for hormonal imbalance contexts, including perimenopause, menopause, and PCOS.
