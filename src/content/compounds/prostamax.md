### 🔹 **What is Prostamax?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from prostate tissue (Khavinson-style oral bioregulator, part of the same Cytomax/Cytogens family as Testagen and Ovagen).
**Mechanism:**

- Signals to support the prostate's own regenerative capacity and normal cellular function rather than supplying exogenous hormone or directly blocking any single receptor.
- Associated in the Russian/Eastern European bioregulator literature with support for prostate tissue health, urinary comfort, and general prostate maintenance, particularly in the context of age-related prostate changes (BPH-type complaints).
- Carries the same organ-specific, tissue-targeted signaling logic as the rest of the Cytomax/Cytogens line — narrow in scope, aimed at the tissue it's derived from.

**Primary focus:**

- Prostate tissue maintenance and support, especially for men experiencing age-related prostate changes.
- General male urogenital comfort and function as part of a broader bioregulator protocol.

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:**

- **100 mcg per capsule**, generally **1–2 capsules, 1–2 times daily**, consistent with dosing conventions for this bioregulator family.

**Frequency:**

- Daily dosing for a minimum course of **20 days**.

**Rationale:** As an oral bioregulator, Prostamax is designed for GI-stable short-peptide signaling to the prostate specifically — the goal is nudging the tissue's own regulatory processes, not delivering a systemic hormonal or pharmacological effect.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** Minimum 20-day course, consistent with other bioregulators in this family.
**Off-cycle:** Reassess symptoms/urinary function between courses before repeating; typically used as a structured short course rather than continuous daily use.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Taken with or around meals per label convention for oral bioregulators.
- Framed as a defined course rather than an ongoing daily supplement.

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral)**

- **Example protocol:** 100 mcg, 1 capsule twice daily, for 20 days.
- Sometimes discussed alongside **Testagen** for a fuller male reproductive/urogenital picture, since the two target adjacent but distinct tissue (prostate vs. testicular).

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Prostamax targets **prostate tissue specifically**, complementary stacks tend to focus on the broader male urogenital and hormonal picture:
🔸 **Male Reproductive Axis Support:**

- **Testagen:** Overlapping male-tissue bioregulator (testicular rather than prostate), sometimes paired for a more complete male-organ-system approach.

🔸 **General Vitality & Recovery:**

- Immune- and metabolism-supportive bioregulators can complement Prostamax's narrow, organ-specific effect without duplicating its mechanism.

**Rationale:** Prostamax's role is tissue-specific — the prostate — so stacking logic centers on either reinforcing the surrounding male reproductive axis or supporting general resilience alongside it.
