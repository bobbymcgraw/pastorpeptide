### 🔹 **What is Bonothyrk?**

**Class:** Short peptide bioregulator derived from parathyroid gland tissue, positioned within the Khavinson-style Russian bioregulator family.
**Mechanism:**

- Functionally echoes parathyroid hormone (PTH) activity: raises serum calcium by promoting bone resorption, boosting intestinal calcium uptake, and cutting renal calcium loss.
- Drives both sides of bone turnover — stimulating osteoclast and osteoblast activity to support ongoing remodeling and repair.
- Helps hold the calcium-phosphate balance steady by reducing calcium excretion while nudging phosphate elimination upward.

**Primary focus:**

- Bone mineral density and osteoporosis-related support, calcium imbalance correction (particularly hypocalcemia), and general fracture/bone-injury recovery.

---

🔹 **Oral Capsule Dosage (primary route)**
**Range:** **21.5 mg per capsule**, typically **1–2 capsules taken 1–2 times daily** with food.
**Typical protocol:** A standard course runs around **30 days**, adjusted up or down based on baseline bone health markers and serum calcium status.
**Rationale:** Oral dosing lets the peptide exert a localized parathyroid-mimicking signal without the complexity of injectable PTH-analog therapy.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**
**Common cycle length:** Roughly 30 days per course.
**Off-cycle:** Extended breaks between cycles are standard, with monitoring of calcium status guiding when to resume.
**Note:** Because this peptide echoes PTH pharmacology, long-term high-dose use carries a theoretical risk profile similar to that seen in prolonged PTH-analog therapies — this is a reason to keep cycles short and monitored rather than continuous.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Taken **with meals** to support absorption and reduce gastrointestinal discomfort.
- Regular monitoring of serum calcium and kidney function is the sensible practice alongside any bone-focused bioregulator course, given the calcium-shifting mechanism at play.

---

✅ **Educational Integration Example (oral capsule)**

- **Example protocol:** 21.5 mg capsule, once or twice daily with food, for a 30-day course, considered in the context of bone density support or post-fracture recovery.
- Often discussed alongside Cartalax for combined bone-and-cartilage joint support.

🔹 **Stacking Strategy (Educational / Complementary)**
Since Bonothyrk centers on **calcium regulation and bone remodeling**, complementary stacking tends to focus on connective tissue and structural support:
🔸 **Structural / Connective Tissue Bioregulators:**

- **Cartalax:** Cartilage-focused bioregulator that pairs conceptually with Bonothyrk's bone-remodeling emphasis for joint and skeletal support.

🔸 **General Recovery Support:**

- **BPC-157:** Broad tissue-repair peptide sometimes considered alongside bone-healing protocols.

**Rationale:** Bonothyrk targets the mineral and remodeling side of bone health; stacking aims to **cover the connective tissue and repair side** without duplicating the calcium-shifting mechanism unnecessarily.
