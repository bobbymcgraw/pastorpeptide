### 🔹 **What is SS-31?**

Class: Synthetic mitochondria-targeted tetrapeptide; also known as Elamipretide, MTP-131, and Bendavia.
Mechanism:

- Binds to **cardiolipin**, a phospholipid in the inner mitochondrial membrane, stabilizing its structure.
- Enhances **electron transfer** in the mitochondrial respiratory chain, reducing **reactive oxygen species (ROS)** production.
- Prevents mitochondrial **permeability transition pore (mPTP)** formation, preserving mitochondrial integrity.
- Improves mitochondrial **membrane potential**, **ATP production**, and **biogenesis**.
- Exhibits **antioxidant** and **anti-inflammatory** properties, mitigating oxidative stress and inflammation.

Primary focus:

- **Mitochondrial dysfunction** and related diseases.
- **Age-related decline** in mitochondrial function.
- **Neurological disorders** (e.g., Alzheimer's, Parkinson's).
- **Cardiovascular diseases** (e.g., heart failure).
- **Metabolic disorders** (e.g., diabetes, obesity).
- **Musculoskeletal diseases** (e.g., mitochondrial myopathy).

---

🔹 **Oral Dosage**

- **Not applicable**; SS-31 has **poor oral bioavailability** and requires **parenteral administration**.

---

🔹 **Injectable / Subcutaneous Dosage (most common)**
Range:

- **0.5–2 mg/kg body weight**, administered **subcutaneously** or **intravenously**.
- **Frequency**: Typically **once daily** or as directed by clinical protocols.

Rationale: Direct administration ensures effective delivery to mitochondria, bypassing absorption barriers.

---

🔹 **Cycle Length**

- **Acute applications**: Single-dose or short-term treatment.
- **Chronic applications**: Cycles ranging from **4 to 12 weeks**, depending on the condition being treated.

---

🔹 **Optimal Dosing Window**

- Administered at **any time of day**, based on clinical protocol.
- For **acute conditions**, dosing may be more frequent, while **chronic conditions** may require less frequent dosing.

⚠️ **Safety Notes**

- **Gastrointestinal side effects** (e.g., nausea, diarrhea) may occur, especially during dose escalation.
- Monitor for signs of **hypoglycemia**, particularly in patients with **type 2 diabetes**.
- Contraindicated in patients with a history of **medullary thyroid carcinoma** or **multiple endocrine neoplasia type 2**.
- Not intended for human or animal use; for **research purposes only**.
