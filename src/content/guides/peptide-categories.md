Peptide Categories & Examples – Quick Reference

Peptides are grouped by their main purpose or effect. Here are some of the most common types you’ll hear about and examples of each.
**1. GLP-1s & Metabolic Peptides**
*(Support blood sugar control, satiety, and weight loss)*

- **Semaglutide** (Ozempic, Wegovy)
- **Tirzepatide** (Mounjaro, Zepbound)
- **Retatrutide**
- **Cagrilintide** *(GLP-1/GIP co-agonist)*

---

**2. Growth Hormone Secretagogues (GHSs)**
*(Signal the body to release more natural growth hormone)*

- **CJC-1295** (with or without DAC)
- **Ipamorelin**
- **Tesamorelin**
- **GHRP-2 / GHRP-6**

---

**3. Healing & Recovery Peptides**
*(Promote tissue repair, reduce inflammation)*

- **BPC-157**
- **TB-500** (Thymosin Beta-4)
- **KPV** *(anti-inflammatory)*

---

**4. Muscle Growth & Performance Peptides**
*(Enhance muscle repair, recovery, and endurance)*

- **IGF-1 LR3**
- **Follistatin-344**
- **AOD-9604** *(fat loss + joint health)*

---

**5. Cognitive & Nootropic Peptides**
*(Support focus, mood, memory, and neuroprotection)*

- **Semax**
- **Selank**
- **Dihexa**
- **Cerebrolysin**

---

**6. Aesthetic & Cosmetic Peptides**
*(Skin, hair, tanning, and anti-aging)*

- **GHK-Cu** *(collagen stimulation, hair growth)*
- **AHK-Cu** *(hair-specific)*
- **Melanotan II** *(tanning)*
- **Snap-8** *(topical anti-wrinkle)*

---

**7. Advanced / Experimental Peptides**
*(Primarily research-phase, cutting-edge uses)*

- **FOXO4-DRI** *(senolytic / anti-aging research)*
- **SS-31** *(mitochondrial function)*
- **Epitalon** *(longevity research)*
- **5-Amino-1MQ** *(metabolism / fat loss)*
