### 🔹 **What is the Enhanced Sleep Stack?**

The Enhanced Sleep Stack combines DSIP, BPC-157, and CJC-1295 (with DAC) to hit sleep from three different angles: inducing deep sleep directly, supporting the systems that regulate the sleep-wake cycle, and boosting the growth hormone release that naturally happens overnight.

This is built for people whose recovery is being bottlenecked by poor sleep — whether that's disrupted sleep architecture, slow physical recovery, or just waking up feeling like nothing repaired overnight.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [DSIP](/compounds/dsip)
- [BPC-157](/compounds/bpc-157)
- [CJC-1295 (with DAC)](/compounds/cjc-1295-with-dac)

**Primary focus:**

- Deepen and stabilize sleep architecture
- Support the body's natural repair processes during rest
- Amplify the growth hormone release that occurs overnight

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **DSIP:** The anchor of the stack for sleep itself — associated with promoting deep, delta-wave sleep and helping regulate the sleep cycle so rest is more consistent and restorative.
- **BPC-157:** Better known for tissue healing, but it also plays a supporting role here through its effect on serotonin, which contributes to a calming effect and helps regulate the sleep-wake cycle. In this stack it's doing double duty — recovery support plus sleep support.
- **CJC-1295 (with DAC):** A long-acting growth hormone-releasing hormone analog that extends GH secretion. Since most natural GH release happens during sleep, pairing it here means better sleep quality can translate into a bigger GH pulse, and that GH in turn supports the body's repair processes overnight.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Deeper, more restorative sleep and improved sleep efficiency
- Faster physical recovery from training or injury
- Sharper cognitive function the next day — memory, focus, problem-solving
- More stable energy levels throughout the day
- Support for immune function, which leans heavily on quality sleep
