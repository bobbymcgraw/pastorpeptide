### 🔹 **What is the Calm Focus Stack?**

The Calm Focus Stack pairs Selank and Semax, two nootropic peptides that are usually talked about separately but work well together because they're solving two sides of the same problem. One takes the edge off, the other sharpens things up. Put together, the goal is a brain that's both calmer and clearer — less noise, more signal.

This one's aimed at people whose performance gets wrecked by stress: students cramming, professionals under deadline pressure, anyone who notices their focus falls apart the moment anxiety creeps in.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [Selank](/compounds/selank)
- [Semax](/compounds/semax)

**Primary focus:**

- Reduce anxiety without sedation
- Sharpen memory, focus, and learning capacity
- Stabilize mood during high-stress periods
- Support long-term brain health

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **Selank:** Works on the anxiety side of the equation. It nudges serotonin activity in a direction that steadies mood and blunts the stress response, and it interacts with the body's natural opioid-calming pathways. In this stack, it's the compound taking the pressure off so the brain has room to focus.
- **Semax:** Works on the performance side. It's tied to increased BDNF activity, which supports neuron health and the kind of learning and memory processes you need for demanding mental work. Where Selank calms, Semax sharpens — together they cover both ends of "calm but capable."

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Better focus, memory retention, and learning capacity
- Lower anxiety and a steadier emotional baseline under stress
- More sustained mental energy without the crash
- Support for long-term neuronal health
