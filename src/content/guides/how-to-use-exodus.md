Exodus is a user-friendly, self-custody crypto wallet that allows you to manage, send, receive, and exchange a wide variety of cryptocurrencies. Here's a quick summary of how to use it:
**1. Getting Started & Security:**

- **Download and Install:** Download the Exodus app for your desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux), mobile device (iOS, Android), or as a browser extension (Chrome, Brave).
- **Self-Custody:** Exodus is a "self-custody" wallet, meaning you have full control over your private keys. Your private keys and transaction data are stored locally on your device and are encrypted.
- **Backup Your Wallet:** The most critical step is backing up your 12-word secret recovery phrase. This phrase is the master key to your funds. Write it down on paper and store it in a secure, offline location. Never store it digitally or share it with anyone.
- **Security:** Set a strong password and consider enabling biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID) for added security. Exodus also integrates with Trezor hardware wallets for an extra layer of protection.

**2. Core Functions:**

- **Receive Crypto:** To receive crypto, go to the wallet for the specific asset you want to receive and click "Receive." This will display your public address and a QR code. Copy the address or use the QR code to have someone send you crypto.
- **Send Crypto:** To send crypto, select the asset you want to send and click "Send." Enter the recipient's address and the amount you want to send. Double-check the address carefully, as blockchain transactions are irreversible. You can also customize transaction fees for some assets.
- **Buy Crypto:** You can buy crypto directly within the Exodus wallet using various third-party providers and payment methods like credit/debit cards, bank transfers, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
- **Swap Crypto:** Exodus has a built-in exchange feature that allows you to swap one cryptocurrency for another without leaving the app. This is done through third-party partners and a small spread fee is included in the transaction.

**3. Additional Features:**

- **Portfolio Tracker:** Exodus provides a real-time view of your portfolio's value across all your assets.
- **Staking:** You can earn passive income by "staking" certain cryptocurrencies directly within the wallet.
- **NFTs:** The wallet also supports storing and displaying NFTs.
