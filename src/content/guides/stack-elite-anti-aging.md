### 🔹 **What is the Elite Anti-Aging Stack?**

The Elite Anti-Aging Stack combines GHK-Cu and Epitalon, two peptides that attack aging from opposite directions — one working on the surface, the other working at the cellular level. GHK-Cu is the skin-and-tissue repair peptide; Epitalon is the one people reach for when they're thinking about cellular aging and telomeres.

This stack is built for people who want a fuller approach to anti-aging than skincare alone — something that addresses both how you look and how your cells are holding up over time.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [GHK-Cu](/compounds/ghk-cu)
- [Epitalon](/compounds/epitalon)

**Primary focus:**

- Improve skin appearance, elasticity, and repair
- Support cellular longevity and healthy aging at the DNA level
- Provide broad antioxidant and neuroprotective support

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **GHK-Cu:** A copper-binding peptide that drives collagen and elastin production, which is what keeps skin firm and resilient. It also functions as an antioxidant, helping offset the free-radical damage that accumulates with age. In this stack, it's doing the visible, outward-facing work.
- **Epitalon:** Linked to telomerase activity, the enzyme involved in maintaining telomere length as cells divide. Shorter telomeres are associated with cellular aging, so supporting this process is the more "under the hood" half of the stack — working on longevity rather than appearance.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Reduced fine lines and improved skin elasticity
- Support for telomere length and cellular lifespan
- Broader antioxidant protection against cellular damage
- Potential neuroprotective effects that support cognitive health with age
- Overall support for vitality as the two mechanisms work together
