### 🔹 **What is the Ultimate Fat Loss Stack?**

This stack brings together AOD-9604, MOTS-C, and Tesamorelin — three peptides that attack fat loss and body composition from different directions instead of one. Rather than relying on a single mechanism, the combination is built to push lipolysis, metabolic efficiency, and growth-hormone-driven fat reduction at the same time, while keeping lean muscle in the picture. It's aimed at people focused on meaningful fat loss and body recomposition, not just a number on the scale.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [AOD-9604](/compounds/aod-9604)
- [MOTS-C](/compounds/mots-c)
- [Tesamorelin](/compounds/tesamorelin)

**Primary focus:**

- Enhancing fat loss through multiple metabolic pathways
- Boosting overall metabolic rate and energy expenditure
- Reducing visceral fat specifically
- Preserving lean muscle mass while losing fat

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **AOD-9604:** Mimics the fat-burning fragment of human growth hormone, encouraging the body to break down stored fat and resist converting excess food into new fat.
- **MOTS-C:** Influences how the body metabolizes fat for fuel and how it responds to exercise, adding a metabolic-efficiency layer to the stack.
- **Tesamorelin:** Raises natural growth hormone release, which is particularly linked to visceral fat reduction while helping support muscle mass.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- A more comprehensive approach to fat loss than any single compound alone
- Improved metabolic rate and more efficient calorie use
- Targeted reduction in visceral fat, which carries outsized health risk
- Preservation of lean muscle mass alongside fat loss
- More energy available for training, thanks to the metabolic boost
