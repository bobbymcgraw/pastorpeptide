### 🔹 **What is the Wolverine Stack?**

Named for the comic-book character famous for shrugging off just about anything, the Wolverine Stack pairs BPC-157 and TB-500 — two of the most talked-about recovery peptides in the space — into one protocol built around a single goal: heal faster. Where either peptide works well on its own, running them together is meant to hit recovery from more angles at once, from local tissue repair to broader systemic resilience. It's a go-to combo for athletes, anyone working through rehab, or anyone dealing with the aftermath of physical trauma who wants to shorten the timeline back to normal.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [BPC-157](/compounds/bpc-157)
- [TB-500](/compounds/tb500)

**Primary focus:**

- Speeding up recovery from injury
- Improving tissue regeneration across muscle, tendon, ligament, and joint
- Reducing inflammation at and around injury sites
- Supporting overall systemic resilience, not just the local injury

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **BPC-157:** Supports the body's natural healing response, especially in soft tissue like muscles, tendons, and ligaments, and encourages angiogenesis — new blood vessel formation that helps deliver nutrients to and clear waste from an injury site.
- **TB-500:** Acts at the cellular level through the actin cytoskeleton, helping cells migrate and proliferate — the basic machinery behind healing, reduced inflammation, and better flexibility in connective tissue.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Faster overall recovery and less downtime after injury
- Better flexibility and mobility, with less adhesion or fibrous buildup in soft tissue
- Broader injury repair across muscle, tendon, joint, and some internal tissue
- Reduced inflammation and swelling that can slow healing down
- Support for overall resilience beyond just the injury site itself
