### 🔹 **What is the Upgraded Glow Stack?**

The Upgraded Glow Stack combines four peptides — BPC-157, GHK-Cu, TB-500, and KPV — into a protocol focused on skin, healing, and cellular rejuvenation rather than any single one of those on its own. Two members of the stack are workhorse healing peptides, one is a well-known collagen-boosting copper peptide, and the fourth brings targeted anti-inflammatory support for the skin itself. Together they're built for anyone chasing a broader anti-aging and skin-health outcome, whether that's recovering from a cosmetic procedure, calming an irritated complexion, or just wanting skin, hair, and nails to hold up better over time.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [BPC-157](/compounds/bpc-157)
- [GHK-Cu](/compounds/ghk-cu)
- [TB-500](/compounds/tb500)
- [KPV](/compounds/kpv)

**Primary focus:**

- Improving skin health, elasticity, and appearance
- Accelerating healing after cosmetic procedures or skin injury
- Calming inflammation and evening out skin tone
- Supporting stronger hair and nails alongside skin benefits

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **BPC-157:** Drives tissue repair and supports gut health, which feeds back into overall skin condition — healthy gut function and healthy skin tend to go together.
- **GHK-Cu:** A copper peptide that ramps up collagen and elastin production, improving skin elasticity and softening the look of fine lines.
- **TB-500:** Promotes healing at the cellular level and improves tissue flexibility, helping the skin bounce back faster from damage or injury.
- **KPV:** Brings targeted anti-inflammatory action to the skin, helpful for conditions like redness, psoriasis, or eczema, and rounds out the stack's calming effect.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Healthier, more resilient skin with a reduction in visible signs of aging
- Faster recovery from cosmetic procedures or unintentional skin damage
- Less inflammation and a more even skin tone and texture
- Improved skin elasticity and hydration from increased collagen and elastin
- Stronger, healthier-looking hair and nails as a secondary benefit
