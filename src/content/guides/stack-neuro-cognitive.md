### 🔹 **What is the Neuro-Cognitive Stack?**

This stack pairs three brain-focused peptides — Pinealon, PE-22-28, and Selank — into a single protocol aimed at long-term brain health rather than a single quick effect. Instead of chasing one lane like memory or mood, the combo works across neuron growth, cellular protection, and emotional regulation at the same time. It's built for people thinking about cognitive longevity: students and professionals who lean on sharp focus day to day, and anyone who wants to get ahead of age-related cognitive decline before it becomes a problem.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [Pinealon](/compounds/pinealon)
- [PE-22-28](/compounds/pe-22-28)
- [Selank](/compounds/selank)

**Primary focus:**

- Supporting long-term brain health and cognitive longevity
- Protecting neurons from stress-related damage
- Encouraging neurogenesis and new neural connections
- Stabilizing mood and reducing anxiety

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **Pinealon:** Works at the gene-expression level to support neural growth and repair, helping keep brain cells functioning well as the body ages.
- **PE-22-28:** Shields neurons from oxidative stress and supports cellular resilience, which is a key piece of preventing age-related cognitive decline.
- **Selank:** Balances neurotransmitter activity to ease anxiety and stress, adding a mental-clarity and emotional-stability layer the other two don't directly cover.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Sharper day-to-day cognitive performance — memory, focus, and problem-solving
- Long-term neuroprotection that supports cognitive longevity
- Encouragement of new neuron growth and neural connections
- Reduced anxiety and more stable mood as part of the overall picture
