Testing Companies

**Janoshik Analytical** – Gold standard for peptide testing. Located in the Czech Republic so factor ~$70 for shipping. They offer HPLC-MS, Endotoxin, Heavy Metals, LCMS and Sterility testing. They are widely used in the research community.
HPLC-MS $300
Additional vial for HPLC-MS $60/vial
Endotoxin $120 (additional vial needed)
Heavy Metals $60 (additional vial needed)
LCMS $170 (additional vial needed)
Sterility $240 (additional vial needed)
**Chromate Analytical** – They are based in the US. They offer HPLC-MS, Endotoxin, Heavy Metals and Sterility testing. They have the ability to test purity in blends as well. They do offer all 4 of the tests listed for $650 per sample
HPLC-MS $300
Additional vial for HPLC-MS $50/vial
Endotoxin $240 (additional vial needed)
Heavy Metals $60
Sterility $240 (additional vial needed)
**Trustpointe** – They are based in the US. They offer HPLC-MS, Endotoxin and Sterility testing. They do not offer heavy metal testing at this time. That is something they may possibly do in the future.
HPLC-MS $200-$400 depends on peptide
Additional vial for HPLC-MS $50/vial
Endotoxin $300 (additional vial needed)
Sterility $250 (additional vial needed)
**MZ Bio Labs** – They are based in the US. They offer HPLC-MS and Endotoxin testing. They do offer two different endotoxin tests and they claim they are good as any sterility test out there.
HPLC-MS $250 and up
Endotoxin $120-$180 (two extra vials needed)
**Forever Pharmacy** – They are based in the US. They offer HPLC-MS and Endotoxin testing. Their endotoxin test is more than other labs do, they perform bacteria and fungal testing with their endotoxins tests and they claim they are good as any sterility test out there.
HPLC-MS $199
Endotoxin $99
**Freedom Diagnostics**- They are based in the US. They offer HPLC-MS and Endotoxin testing. They do not offer heavy metal testing or sterility at this time.
