### 🔹 **What is the Growth Hormone Muscle Building Stack?**

The Growth Hormone Muscle Building Stack pairs CJC-1295 (with DAC) and Ipamorelin — a long-acting GHRH analog paired with a selective GH secretagogue — to drive a stronger, more sustained natural growth hormone response than either would produce on its own.

This is the go-to combination for people whose main goal is muscle growth: more size, faster recovery between sessions, and better overall training output.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [CJC-1295 (with DAC)](/compounds/cjc-1295-with-dac)
- [Ipamorelin](/compounds/ipamorelin)

**Primary focus:**

- Maximize muscle growth and strength
- Shorten recovery time between training sessions
- Support overall athletic performance

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **CJC-1295 (with DAC):** Extends the duration of GH secretion by amplifying the natural pulses released by the pituitary gland, keeping GH levels elevated for longer than a short-acting GHRH would. It's the "duration" half of the stack.
- **Ipamorelin:** A selective GH secretagogue that triggers additional GH release through the ghrelin receptor, without meaningfully affecting cortisol or acetylcholine. It's the "clean pulse" half — adding GH release on top of CJC-1295's extended baseline without unwanted side effects.

- Faster recovery from intense training, allowing more frequent sessions
- Reduced body fat alongside lean muscle gains
