### 🔹 **What is the Growth Hormone Fat Loss Stack?**

The Growth Hormone Fat Loss Stack pairs Tesamorelin and Ipamorelin to push natural growth hormone output higher, with fat loss — especially stubborn visceral fat — as the main target. Tesamorelin is the heavy hitter for visceral fat specifically, while Ipamorelin adds a clean, low-side-effect GH pulse on top.

This stack is aimed at people focused on body composition first: dropping fat, especially around the midsection, while holding onto (or even building) lean muscle in the process.

**Compounds in this stack:**

- [Tesamorelin](/compounds/tesamorelin)
- [Ipamorelin](/compounds/ipamorelin)

**Primary focus:**

- Reduce visceral fat specifically
- Support fat loss while preserving lean muscle
- Boost natural growth hormone output

---

🔹 **How It Works**

- **Tesamorelin:** A GHRH analog with a well-documented reputation for targeting visceral adipose tissue — the deep abdominal fat that's harder to lose through diet and training alone and carries the most metabolic risk. It's the primary fat-loss driver in this stack.
- **Ipamorelin:** A selective GH secretagogue that mimics ghrelin to trigger GH release without meaningfully raising cortisol. That clean profile is exactly why it pairs well with Tesamorelin here — it adds another GH pulse without stacking on unwanted side effects.

---

🔹 **Potential Benefits**

- Targeted reduction of visceral fat
- Support for lean muscle growth and maintenance alongside fat loss
- Faster recovery from training and physical activity
- Support for cellular repair — skin, bone, and general tissue health
- Some offset of age-related changes like skin laxity and low energy
